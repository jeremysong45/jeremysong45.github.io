Renhe Restaurant

Location: Shanghai

Visited: May 2025

Rating: 17/20

Review Summary

Renhe Restaurant impressed me with its modern take on regional Chinese flavors. The tasting menu included intricate textures and artful plating without feeling overly conceptual. The standout dish was a braised abalone with sea cucumber in a rich mushroom broth—perfectly executed.

Ambiance

The space was elegant but not stiff—warm lighting, understated decor, and very attentive service without being invasive.

Would I Return?

Absolutely. It’s a gem in Shanghai’s fine dining scene that I think deserves even more attention internationally.
