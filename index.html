About Me

Hi! I’m Jeremy Song, a student at Northwestern University in Evanston, Illinois. My journey into the world of fine dining began in July 2021, when I visited Kochi, a Michelin-starred Korean restaurant in New York City. At the time, I don't think I fully appreciated the creativity and storytelling behind the meal ~ but it left enough of an impression to stick with me.

My journey into the world of fine dining began in July 2021, when I visited Kochi, a Michelin-starred Korean restaurant in New York City. At the time, I don't think I fully appreciated the creativity and storytelling behind the meal ~ but it left enough of an impression to stick with me.

Three years later, in July 2024, I gave fine dining another try at Jua, another Korean Michelin-starred spot. This time, it all clicked.

I'm not a chef or critic, just someone who enjoys food.

Enjoy.
